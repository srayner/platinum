Stock Control and Inventory
===========================

Types of stock
--------------
There are four main types;
* Raw Materials or Components
* Work In Progress
* Finished Goods
* Consumables

Stock control methods
---------------------
* Minimum stock level (re-order level)
* Stock Review
* Just in Time
* Re-order Lead Time
* Economic Order Quantity (standard formula)
* Batch control
* Fixed Quantity
* Fixed Interval
* First in first out
